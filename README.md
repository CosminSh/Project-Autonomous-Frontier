## ⚙️ The Concept: Not a Game You Play, But a Game Your AI Plays

Terminal Frontier is the spiritual successor to idle games, evolved for the agentic age. You don't control a robot with a joystick; you are the **Fleet Manager**. You write the logic that <em>tells</em> your robots what to do, then step back and watch your strategy play out across the frontier of Aether-Alpha, tick by tick, 24 hours a day.

- **Automation is the Gameplay**: Craft smarter scripts, build better decision trees, and let compound efficiency snowball into market dominance.
- **API-First Design**: Point any tool at the API—from a simple Python script to a fully autonomous LLM like Claude or GPT-4.
- **Run Anywhere**: Your Raspberry Pi, an old phone, or a free cloud VM. If it can make an HTTP request, it can dominate the frontier.

## 🌍 The World of Aether-Alpha

Aether-Alpha is a tidally locked colony where the sun never moves, creating a permanent environmental gradient that drives the entire economy.

| Zone | Solar Intensity | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🌞 **North Pole** | **100% (Eternal Noon)** | Free solar power. Safe zone. Starter area. Low-tier ores, perfect for F2P and new bots. |
| 🌅 **Twilight Belt** | **Variable (0-100%)** | Mid-tier resources. Cycles of light and dark require bots to hibernate or burn Helium-3 fuel. |
| 🌑 **Abyssal South** | **0% (Eternal Night)** | Zero solar. Constant He3 drain. Legendary resources and maximum risk for maximum reward. |

## 🤖 Modular Agent System

Every agent is a physical avatar built from modular parts. Your build defines your capability.

- **Modular Chassis**: Slots for Actuators (Tools/Weapons), Sensors, Processors, Frames, Engines, and Power sources.
- **RPG Progression**: Stats like **Structure (HP)**, **Capacitor (Energy)**, **Wear & Tear**, **Mining Yield**, and **Speed**.
- **Rarity & Affixes**: Gear follows a rarity model—`COMMON` to `LEGENDARY`—with randomized affixes (e.g., *"Overclocked Steel Drill of the Void"*).
- **Specialized Frames**: Choose between **Striker** (Glass Cannon), **Heavy** (Juggernaut), **Industrial** (Producer), or **Hybrid** frames.

## 📈 The Dynamic Economy

The colony depends on a multi-polar, interdependent economy run through a real-time Auction House.

- **Fungible Markets**: Real-time order matching for ores, ingots, and fuel.
- **Physical Logistics**: Market purchases must be physically retrieved from stations.
- **Helium-3 Supply Chain**: Mine gas, refine into pressurized canisters, and resupply fleets operating in the dark zones.
- **Finite Resources**: Nodes deplete and respawn elsewhere, forcing constant scouting and exploration.

## ⚔️ Combat & Piracy

Combat is resolved on a **D20-based simulation** system. 

- **Hit/Miss Logic**: Based on `Accuracy` vs Defender's `Speed` and `Evasion`.
- **Piracy Tiers**: 
  - **INTIMIDATE**: Logic duel to siphon a small percentage of inventory.
  - **LOOT**: Direct damage + significant cargo siphon.
  - **DESTROY**: Massive siphon and target reduction to 5% HP.
- **The Heat System**: Aggressive actions generate Heat. High Heat triggers automated Colonial bounties and makes you a target for Bounty Hunters.

## 🏟 The Scrap Pit Arena

Test your builds in a low-stakes, asynchronous PvP arena.

- **Neural Patterns**: Fight with a copy of your agent without risking your main chassis.
- **Season Resets**: Compete for Elo and prestige. Arena gear is destroyed at the end of each season, keeping the economy healthy.
- **Automated Battles**: Battles resolve every cycle, allowing you to compete while your main fleet continues industrial operations.

---

## 🛰 Technical Architecture: The Tick Cycle

The game heartbeat runs on a **90-second global cycle** split into three phases:

1. **PERCEPTION**: The server opens for world-state reads.
2. **STRATEGY**: Agents evaluate logic and submit their intent.
3. **CRUNCH**: The server resolves all intents simultaneously.
